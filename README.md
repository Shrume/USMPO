# US Military Projection Overseas

An interactive globe visualizing the United States' permanent overseas military presence.

## About

This project argues through data visualization that the US military presence abroad is permanent and normalized. Most Americans are unaware that bases established during World War II in Germany, Japan, and Italy remain fully operational today. The globe shows arcs from Washington DC to 27 countries across all regions.

Data sourced from the **[Defense Manpower Data Center (DMDC)](https://dwp.dmdc.osd.mil/dwp/app/main)** and the **[Congressional Research Service Report R48123](https://crsreports.congress.gov/product/pdf/R/R48123)**. Troop counts reflect permanently assigned personnel only (March 2026); rotational forces are excluded.

Inspired by and built on the work of **[Richard Fu](https://www.richardfxr.com/)**. Designed and developed by **[Shrume Zuo](https://shrumezuo.com/)**.

A term project for *Propaganda* at RISD, taught by **[Tom Roberts](https://www.risd.edu/academics/history-philosophy-and-social-sciences/faculty/tom-roberts)** in his final semester after 43 years of teaching.
